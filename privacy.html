Privacy Policy

SOL Korean BBQ (soldhaka.com) — Last updated July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SOL Korean BBQ ("SOL", "we", "us"), located at 12th Floor, Ventura Mall, Road 103, Gulshan 2, Dhaka-1212, Bangladesh, handles information when you visit soldhaka.com or message us on Facebook Messenger or Instagram, including through our automated concierge assistant.

Information we receive

When you message our Facebook Page or Instagram account, we (and our automated assistant) receive:

  • The text of the message you send
  • Your Page-scoped or Instagram-scoped identifier, provided by Meta so we can reply to the correct conversation — this is not your regular Facebook/Instagram account ID and cannot be used to look up your profile
  • Basic delivery metadata Meta provides with every message (timestamp, message ID)

We do not ask for or knowingly collect payment details, government ID numbers, or other sensitive personal data through Messenger or Instagram.

How we use it

Your message is used only to generate a relevant reply — answering questions about our menu, hours, BBQ cuts, reservations, and location. This happens in one of two ways:

  • Most questions are answered directly by rule-based logic using our own published menu and restaurant facts — no third-party AI model is involved.
  • For open-ended questions our rules don't cover, the message text (and the restaurant facts needed to answer it) may be sent to OpenAI's API to generate a reply. OpenAI processes this text solely to produce that one response and does not receive any other information about you.

We do not use your messages for advertising, and we do not sell or rent your information to any third party.

Who processes this data on our behalf

  • Meta Platforms, Inc. — operates Facebook Messenger and Instagram, the channels through which messages reach us. Meta's own privacy policy governs how it handles your account and message data on its platforms.
  • n8n — the automation service that routes your message to our reply system and back to Messenger/Instagram. n8n retains a short operational execution log for our account, used only for debugging.
  • Vercel — hosts the web service that generates replies.
  • OpenAI — only for open-ended questions, as described above.

Retention

We do not maintain our own long-term database of guest messages. Conversation history is retained only as far as Facebook Messenger, Instagram, and our automation provider's standard operational logs retain it, for debugging and service reliability.

Reservations made by phone, WhatsApp, or our booking page

If you reserve a table with us, we collect your name, phone number, and party size to hold and manage your reservation, and to contact you about that reservation. This information is used only for that purpose and is not shared with third parties beyond what's needed to send WhatsApp/SMS confirmations.

Your choices

You can stop messaging our Page or Instagram account at any time. To ask what information we hold about you, or to request it be deleted, email us at sol.restaurant.bd@gmail.com.

Children

Our services are intended for adults making dining decisions and are not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our services change. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
